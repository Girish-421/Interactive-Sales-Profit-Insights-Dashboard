# Interactive-Sales-Profit-Insights-Dashboard

This project showcases an interactive dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends across multiple years. The dashboard enables quick decision-making by transforming raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights using intuitive visualizations.

## Sales Performance Insights

1. Tracks multi-year sales trends to identify growth phases and slowdowns.

2. Reveals seasonal patterns through month-wise sales analysis.

3. Highlights product categories with consistently strong revenue contribution.

## Profitability Insights

1. Shows that profit contribution varies across categories despite similar sales levels.

2. Identifies categories with improving or declining profitability over time.

3. Highlights the outsized profit impact of a small group of customers.

## Customer & Category Behavior

1. Tracks changes in customer distribution to analyze retention and acquisition trends.

2. Identifies top-performing customers for targeted marketing strategies.

3. Compares categories to distinguish volume-driven versus profit-driven products.

## Regional Insights

Reveals significant variation in sales performance across regions.

Identifies consistently high-performing states for focused business expansion.
